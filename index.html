Attackers adapt. Signals decay. Enforcement must scale — while staying fair to legitimate users.

Behavior first. Signals second. Precision always.

Quietly eliminating dishonesty, waste, and stupidity — without lighting the whole platform on fire.

Real Work, Real Systems

When Investigators Kept Making the Same Mistake

Repetitive errors caused by missing context and manual assignment.

Investigators were repeatedly misreading similar transaction patterns. I helped design a small auto-alert assignment logic, clarified SOP language, and added context so the story was visible earlier.

Result: fewer repeat errors, faster reviews, and less back-and-forth.

Tools: SQL, alert queues, SOP docs

Why Region-Wise Policies Were Slowing Everyone Down

Fragmented rules creating confusion and inconsistency.

Region-specific policies caused investigators to second-guess decisions. I helped migrate these into a centralized structure, worked through stakeholder concerns, got approvals, and presented the changes globally.

Result: clearer decisions and consistent enforcement.

Tools: Policy docs, stakeholder decks, governance systems

Stopping Humans from Doing Mechanical Work

Judgment wasted on repetitive workflows.

Investigators were spending time on steps that added no real signal. I helped identify where light automation and better handoffs could reduce friction.

Result: more time spent on judgment, less on repetition.

Tools: Workflow tools, process mapping
